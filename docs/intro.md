# Introduction to Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the PhysicalAI Humanoid Robotics Textbook. This comprehensive guide explores the fascinating world of humanoid robotics, covering fundamental concepts, advanced control systems, and practical applications.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this textbook, you will:
- Understand the fundamentals of robotics and humanoid robot design
- Master kinematics and dynamics principles for robotic motion
- Learn about control systems and sensor integration
- Explore AI and machine learning applications in robotics
- Gain insights into motion planning and locomotion strategies
- Discover real-world applications and future directions

## About This Textbook

This textbook combines theoretical foundations with practical examples to provide a comprehensive understanding of humanoid robotics. Each chapter includes exercises and projects to reinforce your learning.
